Slack Overflow

Slack Overflow was my solo project at Prime Digital Academy and my biggest 'baby' to date. The site, like Stack Overflow, allows users to search for and ask about common development questions. The application is targeted toward current Prime students to address questions that are most specific to Prime assignments, technologies and 'problems'. Technologies I used in the app were AngularJS, Node, Bootstrap, dirPagination, SQL, Express, ng tags input, sweetalert, dirPagination, Filestack, Heroku and Passport. The next phase of Slack Overflow is to integrate Slack slash commands.

Photo Gallery

My Photo Gallery app displays photos and allows a user to add a photo, delete photos, comment on photos, and like photos. My next step for this app would be to add authentication. The technologies I used in this application are AngularJS, bootstrap, Node, Express, and SQL.

A Diamond in the Ruff

A diamond in the Ruff was built for a local business owner, Christina, in Eureka, Montana. The application allows Christina to share a bit about her services, herself and her contact information. The website also displays her release form. Before the website her customers would have to drive to her home to pick up and fill out a release form before they could leave their dog with her!
